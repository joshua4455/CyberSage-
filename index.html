Threat Brief

Know what to look for before an attack finds you.

Phishing Indicators

🚩 Sender domain doesn't match display name

🚩 Urgency language ("Act now", "Expires soon")

🚩 Requests for credentials or MFA codes

🚩 URLs that mismatch on hover

🚩 Generic or slightly wrong name

Social Engineering

🎭 Impersonating authority (CEO, IT, HR)

🎭 Artificial urgency or fear creation

🎭 Using real context to build trust

🎭 Pretexting — fabricated scenarios

🎭 Quid pro quo — offering help for access

Vishing Attacks

📞 Caller ID spoofing

📞 IT helpdesk asking for your password

📞 Pressure before you can verify

📞 Personal details used to build false trust

Your Defense

✅ Verify identity through a separate channel

✅ Never share passwords or MFA codes

✅ Report suspicious comms to security team

✅ When in doubt — escalate, not your fault

✅ Hover links before clicking
